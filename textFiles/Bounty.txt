1. Player enters Bounty (stocked after Journey or Story missions are entered)
2. Display missions to choose from - (Preset: 1 Easy 1 Medium 1 Hard) & Random: (1 Medium 1 Hard)
3. Player chooses mission
  a. select and push range of rooms from journey difficulty
  b. push specific boss room to the end of mission
4. Player will progress through random rooms to a pre-determined boss room
5. Mission is complete when final room is cleared and player leaves area.

Note: Bounty is for collecting trophies(text based) and pre-determined reward.
